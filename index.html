About the university

Built around the student, not the syllabus.

RIMT University is a leading institution in Punjab, spread across a 25-acre campus in Mandi Gobindgarh, offering diverse programs in engineering, management, agriculture, pharmacy, law, and sciences. Recognized among affordable colleges and emerging among the Top 10 colleges in India for career-focused education, the university regularly updates its curriculum to match evolving industry demands.

Smaller batch sizes for lab-heavy subjects, faculty who still consult in industry, and a placement cell that starts working with third-year students rather than final-year ones — that's the practical difference students notice.

Industry-linked curriculum

Syllabi co-designed with hiring partners, updated yearly.

Residential campus

On-site hostels, library, sports complex, and health centre.

Scholarship support

Merit and need-based waivers assessed at entry and renewed yearly.

Placement cell

Dedicated sector-wise recruitment drives from year three onward.
